About Us

Vedanta Fab Works

A multi-product, multi facility and multiservice providing company.

The origin and ascent of VEDANTA Group began in the year 2018 by two technocrats with a small manufacturing setup for Power Electronic Products. Today it has enriched itself into a multi-product, multi facility and multi service providing Company. Vedanta is the only integrated player in the world manufacturing Substation Structure & Line Structure, Lighting Pole Telecom Towers, High Tensile & Mild Steel Fasteners, Overhead Distribution Line Hardware and Accessories, Hot Rolled (HighTensile and Mild) Structural Steel under one banner, offering Industry a one stop solution for most items in T&D segment.
